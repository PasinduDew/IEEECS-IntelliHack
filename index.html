IntelliHack 2K19

About the Event

IntelliHack 2k19 is the first ever local Machine Learning Hackathon and is organized by the IEEE CS Student Branch Chapter of the University of Colombo School of Computing (UCSC). The hackathon would be a 24-hour overnight competition organized for the chosen teams from universities island-wide shortlisted by an experienced panel of judges.

The competitors and other interested undergraduates are exposed to the concept and field of machine learning through 3 workshops prior to the hackathon itself. They would have the privilege of networking with some of the top Machine Learning experts in the industry during the workshops and on the day of the hackathon.

10 Teams Competing
4 Members Each
24 Hours

About Us

The IEEE Computer Society Student Branch Chapter of UCSC is a technical and student chapter from IEEE, it dedicates itself to make progress on the areas related to computing. Its main aim is to be a community that provides technical information, resources, personal development opportunities to its members. Since the day of establishment, it has been organizing many activities and sessions to enhance professional development and technological knowledge of students.

It has played a major role in promoting leadership, organization and interaction skills in addition to improving technical and computing skills among students. The society has conducted several sessions which were helpful for the students to learn new technologies, interact with professionals in the industry, and discover new trends in the field of computing.

VENUE

University of Colombo School of Computing

#35, Reid Ave, Colombo 07

The University of Colombo School of Computing (UCSC) is a higher educational institute affiliated to the University of Colombo in Sri Lanka providing Computer Science and Information and Communication Technology education. The UCSC was formed on 1 September 2002 by bringing together the Department of Computer Science of the Faculty of Science of the University of Colombo and the Institute of Computer Technology and considered as the leading computing higher educational institution in Sri Lanka.
